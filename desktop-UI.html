CULINARY JOURNEY

With Chef Juanma & J Balvin

June 15, 2025 • Miami, Florida

EVENT HIGHLIGHTS

A Night of Culinary Excellence

Over 200 guests experienced Chef Juanma's innovative Colombian cuisine paired with exclusive cocktails and live performances. The five-course menu featured reimagined traditional dishes that showcased the rich culinary heritage of Colombia.

Exclusive Performance

J Balvin delivered an intimate performance of his greatest hits, celebrating Colombian music and culture. The Grammy-winning artist created a special atmosphere that perfectly complemented the evening's celebration of Colombian heritage.

Fundraising Success

The charity auction raised over $250,000 for Vibra en Alta Foundation's educational initiatives in Colombia. Guests bid on exclusive experiences, limited-edition artwork, and one-of-a-kind collaborations between Chef Juanma and J Balvin.

FEATURED PERSONALITIES

Chef Juan Manuel Barrientos

Founder & Executive Chef of El Cielo

Award-winning chef known for his innovative neurogastronomy approach, combining Colombian traditions with cutting-edge techniques. His restaurants have earned Michelin stars and international acclaim for their unique sensory experiences and commitment to Colombian heritage.

Chef Juanma's vision extends beyond the culinary world through his peace initiatives and commitment to sustainability. His work has been recognized globally for both its gastronomic excellence and social impact.

J Balvin

Global Music Icon & Philanthropist

Grammy-winning artist and cultural ambassador who uses his platform to promote Colombian culture worldwide while supporting social causes through his philanthropic work. Known as the "Prince of Reggaeton," J Balvin has transformed Latin music's global presence.

Through the Vibra en Alta Foundation, he focuses on empowering Colombian communities through education, arts, and sustainable development initiatives that preserve cultural heritage while creating opportunities for future generations.
