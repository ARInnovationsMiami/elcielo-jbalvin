CULINARY JOURNEY

With Chef Juanma & J Balvin

June 15, 2025 • Miami, Florida

EVENT HIGHLIGHTS

A Night of Culinary Excellence

Over 200 guests experienced Chef Juanma's innovative Colombian cuisine paired with exclusive cocktails and live performances.

Exclusive Performance

J Balvin delivered an intimate performance of his greatest hits, celebrating Colombian music and culture.

Fundraising Success

The charity auction raised over $250,000 for Vibra en Alta Foundation's educational initiatives in Colombia.

FEATURED PERSONALITIES

Chef Juan Manuel Barrientos

Founder & Executive Chef of El Cielo

Award-winning chef known for his innovative neurogastronomy approach, combining Colombian traditions with cutting-edge techniques. His restaurants have earned Michelin stars and international acclaim.

J Balvin

Global Music Icon & Philanthropist

Grammy-winning artist and cultural ambassador who uses his platform to promote Colombian culture worldwide while supporting social causes through his philanthropic work.
