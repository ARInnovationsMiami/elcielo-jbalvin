THE COLOMBIAN COFFEE STORY

Colombia's coffee is renowned worldwide for its exceptional quality, distinctive flavor profiles, and the rich cultural heritage behind its cultivation. At El Cielo, we celebrate this legacy through our exclusive coffee experiences.

Chef Juan Manuel Barrientos has personally selected the finest beans from Colombia's most prestigious growing regions, working directly with farmers who share our commitment to excellence, sustainability, and tradition.

Chef Juanma visiting coffee farms in the Colombian highlands

DISTINGUISHED REGIONS

Huila

Known for bright acidity, full body, and notes of tropical fruit

Nariño

Distinctive for its sweet profile, citrus notes, and delicate herbal undertones

Santander

Characterized by chocolate notes, medium acidity, and caramel sweetness
