THE COLOMBIAN COFFEE STORY

Colombia's coffee is renowned worldwide for its exceptional quality, distinctive flavor profiles, and the rich cultural heritage behind its cultivation. At El Cielo, we celebrate this legacy through our exclusive coffee experiences.

Chef Juan Manuel Barrientos has personally selected the finest beans from Colombia's most prestigious growing regions, working directly with farmers who share our commitment to excellence, sustainability, and tradition.

Our coffee program represents a journey through Colombia's diverse terroirs, showcasing the unique characteristics that emerge from different altitudes, microclimates, and processing methods.

Chef Juanma visiting coffee farms in the Colombian highlands

DISTINGUISHED REGIONS

Huila

Known for bright acidity, full body, and notes of tropical fruit. Grown at elevations between 1,500-2,000 meters in rich volcanic soil.

Nariño

Distinctive for its sweet profile, citrus notes, and delicate herbal undertones. Cultivated on steep slopes at some of Colombia's highest elevations.

Santander

Characterized by chocolate notes, medium acidity, and caramel sweetness. Traditional farming methods passed down through generations create its distinctive profile.
